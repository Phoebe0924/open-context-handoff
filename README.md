# OCH — Open Context Handoff

> Stop retelling the same story every time you open a new AI chat.

---

## The problem

Every time you switch AI:

- ChatGPT → Claude
- Claude → Gemini
- or just a new chat window

You lose context.

You have to explain again:
- what you're doing
- what already decided
- what should NOT be changed

Even worse:

👉 The AI silently rebuilds your story  
👉 Sometimes incorrectly  
👉 You lose control of what is “true”

---

## The core pain

> Every new AI window feels like starting from zero.

Or worse:

> It feels like reincarnation.

---

## What OCH does

OCH creates a **Context Snapshot** you can carry across AI tools.

It lets you:

- finish a conversation
- generate a snapshot
- paste into a new AI window
- continue immediately

No re-explaining.

---

## How it works (V0)

### 1. Generate snapshot
At the end of a conversation

### 2. Quick confirm (5 seconds)
Check if it looks correct

### 3. Paste into new AI
Start from NEXT ACTION

---

## Snapshot format

```markdown
## OCH Snapshot

WHAT WE'RE DOING
...

WHERE WE ARE
Done:
Current:

HARD DECISIONS
- ...

NEXT ACTION
- ...

CONTEXT NOTES
- ...
```

---

## Principle

> AI drafts.  
> Human approves.  
> Context belongs to the user.

---

## Not for

- memory systems
- agents
- automation
- knowledge bases

Just one thing:

> Help you continue work without repeating yourself.

---

Use it for 3 days across ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini.

If it doesn't reduce repetition → it's wrong.
